I am a graduate student at University of Maryland, College Park where I am specialising in Signal Processing with a focus on Machine Learning techniques. I am interested in working at the intersection of neural signal processing and statistical ML and signal processing techniques. I am especially intrigued by vision and audio models of our brain with cross modality interaction and sensory processing mechanisms being an avid area of focus.

I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers in the past. I did my bachelor thesis on designing circularly polarised antennas for 5G systems and was advised by Prof Asok De. I also interned at various places. I have accumulated industry experience through my internship at Bharat Electronics where I worked on defense radar systems and researched at IIT Delhi as well as Delhi Technological University.

I graduated with a B.Tech (First Class with Distinction) in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Delhi Technological University, Delhi, India in 2021 . For more details, check my CV or hit me up on my email.
